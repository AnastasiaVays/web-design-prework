Jaguars

Few interesting things about Jaguars are:

  • 1. Jaguars enjoy tropical climates and surrounding forests
  • 2. They play an important role in stabilizing ecosystems
  • 3.These beautiful predators are largely represented in Mayan and Aztec mythology
  • 4.The Jaguar is thought to have evolved from the Panthera species ancestor about 1.5 million years ago

Jaguars are strong and mighty predators with an opportunistic nature.
